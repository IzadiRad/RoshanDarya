Who We Are

Roshan Darya Pars Shipping Line, founded in 2018, initially provided forwarding services to customers. Under the leadership of our CEO, Mr. Farid Ahmadi, RDPL has expanded to offer liner services as a carrier and NVOCC across Europe, Australia, Canada, China, India, and the Persian Gulf. We deliver regular and direct services to our valued customers worldwide. Our professional staff is committed to assisting clients in moving their goods globally, making RDPL the best choice for comprehensive logistics management.
